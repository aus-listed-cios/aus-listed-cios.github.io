Quoted Australian Company Issued Options

What follows is a list of tradeable Australian company-issued options, with their strikes and expiry dates. The maintainer offers no guarantee of its accuracy, completeness or fitness for any purpose whatsoever. For a discussion of what a CIO typically is, Tim Rosser has written an excellent summary over at rosser.com.au.

This page could be considered to be a community effort. The data here can and will go stale (eg. in the case of consolidations / splits which the maintainer missed or hasn't yet verified), so please reach out if you notice an error. Feel free to address any enquiries or feedback by email to aus-listed-cios@pm.me. If an exercise price looks too good to be true, it probably is.

Last updated on 21/05/2023. For updates as they happen (more or less), you can follow @aus_listed_cios on twitter.
